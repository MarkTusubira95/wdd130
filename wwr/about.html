Thrills on the River

Our mission is simple: to provide safe, unforgettable, and exhilarating white water rafting experiences. We believe in connecting people with nature's raw power, creating memories that last a lifetime, all while upholding the highest standards of safety and sustainability. Come join our family for an adventure!

History

Founded in 1995 by two passionate river guides, White Water Rafting started with just two inflatable rafts and a deep love for the Great River Nile. Over the past three decades, we've grown into a premier outfitter, recognized for our commitment to environmental stewardship and our top-rated safety record. We are proud to continue the tradition of sharing the rush of the river with every guest.
