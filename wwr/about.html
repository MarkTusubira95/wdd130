White Water Rafting

Our core mission is to share the thrill of white water rafting safely and memorably. We believe in connecting people with the raw power of nature, creating life-long memories, while maintaining the highest commitment to safety and environmental sustainability. Come join us for your next great adventure on the river!

History

Founded in 1995 by two passionate river guides, White Water Rafting started with just two inflatable rafts and a deep love for the Great River Nile. Over the past three decades, we've grown into a premier outfitter, recognized for our commitment to environmental stewardship and our top-rated safety record.

We are proud to continue the tradition of sharing the rush of the river with every guest. Our team is dedicated to preserving the rivers we love, and a portion of every trip fee goes directly toward local conservation efforts to protect the Great River Nile for future generations.
