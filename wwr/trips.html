Our River Adventures

Choose your level of adrenaline. We offer everything from family-friendly floats to intense, multi-day expeditions.

Book Your Adventure Now!

Half-Day Rush

A 3-hour beginner-friendly run with class II-III rapids. Perfect for a quick blast of fun near the source of the Nile. All gear provided.

  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Duration: 3 Hours
  • Ages: 12+

The Full Day Extreme

Our most popular trip. 12 miles of continuous class IV and V rapids, non-stop action, and a riverside lunch. This requires stamina!

  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Duration: 6-7 Hours
  • Ages: 18+

Multi-Day Expedition

A deep-gorge 3-day trip. Experience the river's tranquility and its intensity. Includes all gourmet meals, high-quality tents, and guided hikes.

  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Duration: 3 Days, 2 Nights
  • Ages: 18+

The Family Float

A gentle, guided section perfect for young children or those who prefer sightseeing. Safe, slow, and scenic.

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Duration: 4 Hours
  • Ages: 6+
